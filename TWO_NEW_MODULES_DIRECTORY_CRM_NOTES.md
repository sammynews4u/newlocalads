# Two New Modules Added: Merchant Directory and Lead Management CRM

This update builds on the sixteen-module Local Ads version and adds two more commercial modules.

## 1. Merchant & Business Directory

New route:

- `/merchant-directory`

Purpose:

- Creates a verified business profile layer for advertisers and local merchants.
- Helps Local Ads become more than a click marketplace by giving users a searchable directory of real businesses.
- Supports niche and location discovery for sectors such as real estate, construction, hotels, professional services and local businesses.

Key additions:

- Verified merchant profile logic.
- Directory category explanation.
- Business profile contents.
- Discovery logic by niche, location, offer and trust signal.
- Trust rules for verification and admin control.
- Rollout plan for serious advertisers first.

## 2. Lead Management CRM

New route:

- `/lead-management-crm`

Purpose:

- Helps advertisers track leads created from campaign traffic.
- Moves Local Ads beyond raw clicks into enquiry quality, follow-up discipline and business outcomes.

Key additions:

- Lead sources such as form enquiries, WhatsApp clicks, phone calls and landing actions.
- Pipeline stages from new lead to contacted, qualified, proposal sent, won or lost.
- CRM feature set for advertisers.
- Lead quality rules.
- Automation ideas for reminders, duplicate detection and high-intent escalation.

## Navigation and Homepage Updates

Updated:

- Homepage module count from sixteen to eighteen.
- Homepage module grid now includes Merchant & Business Directory and Lead Management CRM.
- Main marketing navigation now includes Directory and Lead CRM.
- Footer now links to both new modules.

## Strategic Reason

These modules are important because an ad network that only sells clicks is weak. Local Ads needs to help advertisers see actual business opportunities and help verified local businesses become discoverable. The directory creates public trust, while the CRM connects campaign spend to lead outcomes.
